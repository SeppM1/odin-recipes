Mint Chicken

Description

Someones favourite family recipe that their mom has made for hundreds of guests. Fresh lemon juice and mint leaves are a must! Serve over hot cooked white rice

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless,boneless chicken breast halves- cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup margarine
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh mint leaves
